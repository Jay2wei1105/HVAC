## Design Context

### Users
Internal engineering and data analysis users who review HVAC import and training results after onboarding. They want to confirm whether the model is usable, which signals matter, and where to investigate next.

### Brand Personality
Professional, clear, and dashboard-like with a practical engineering tone. The page should feel trustworthy, concise, and easy to scan.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light dashboard style with clean hierarchy, compact summary cards, and readable charts. Avoid over-decoration; keep the interface informative and calm.

### Design Principles
- Lead with a short plain-language conclusion before detailed metrics.
- Show only a few key scores so the result is readable at a glance.
- Combine power and Q feature importance into one overview, but keep the model sources visible.
- Use bars and proportions for feature importance instead of raw tables or dense JSON.
- Keep the page professional and compact, optimized for internal technical review.
